Why is it important to learn technology?

Preparing for the Future: Our world is increasingly driven by technology. Understanding it is essential for future careers. Many jobs, even those not traditionally considered "tech jobs," require digital literacy and technological skills. Learning about technology helps students adapt to the ever-evolving digital landscape.
